Editor’s Review

As the name suggests, StdSoulMates.com strives to bring together people having a sexually transmitted infection for fun and dating, thereby giving them an opportunity to not only seek support from other like – minded people but also find a life companion. The website proves to be a feature – rich platform that is absolutely free to register and use. Regardless of whether you’re looking for a honest relationship or just wish to get some information pertaining to STDs like HSV and HPV, this is the ideal platform to explore.

First Impressions

The website is certainly not the best when it comes to aesthetic appeal. If you’re looking for a well – designed STD dating site, PositiveSingles.com would have been a better option. However, we did find the website to have a fair deal of information pertaining to sexually transmitted infections. However, as all the information is scattered on the home page, it doesn’t look neat and organized. Moreover, the website has more content than it has images, which isn’t necessarily what people find user – friendly.

Features

Apart from the conventional features that are available on every dating site today, there are a few ways in which this site manages to stand out from the crowd. Some of the other interesting features that give this site a unique appeal include:

·No subscription options, meaning this site is free to use for all.
·Simple and quick registering process, which allows users to furnish a great deal of information.
·Read various news articles pertaining to sexually transmitted infections.
·View photos of other users and initiate a conversation to know them better.
·Check out latest research articles on HIV, HSV and other sexually transmitted infections.
·Read articles that have been contributed by experts on how you could deal with such infections.
·Search for people that are interested in certain activities using the tags cloud available on the home page.
·Find a match based on a host of parameters such as age, location and username, among others.

Subscription

StdSoulMates.com is absolutely free of charge. This means, unlike other websites that have a subscription based system wherein users have to pay a fee in order to unlock all options; you wouldn’t have to spend a cent to access all features.

Verdict

StdSoulMates.com is one of the best STD dating sites that can be used absolutely free of charge. Although the site cannot match up to the likes of PositiveSingles.com in terms of both membership base as well as feature set. Nevertheless, when it comes to providing information pertaining to herpes, HPV and other sexually transmitted infections, you could learn a lot on this site.

The website also boasts of a dedicated blog section where you’d be able to communicate with a larger audience. While you’d find a lot of non-genuine users and fake profiles, it isn’t a bad option nonetheless, costing absolutely nothing.
